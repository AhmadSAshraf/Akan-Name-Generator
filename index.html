Akan Name Generator

Welcome to the Akan name generator!!

This is a web application that captures a users date of birth and gender,
determines the day of the week that they were born and assigns an Akan name with respect to that day and their gender.
Akan names come from Ghanian culture where babies are named after the day that they are born.

Enter your birthday and gender to find out what your Akan name is!
